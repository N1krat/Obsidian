_**Introduction to the CyberSecurity**_
	It is a field of the computer science that specifies in the protection of the computer systems, networks from threats such as unauthorized access, data theft, or damage to crucial infrastructure in a company or a nation or just creating chaos in a system, company or a nation.

The growing importance of the cybersecurity began by the aparition of the internet and the development of network standarts by creating sofisticated networks with multiple devices such as phones, pcs, servers or even smart home appliances that can be accessed wireless via Internet.

Such systems as _power grids, electoral processeses and finance_ are the most suseptible to cyber attacks that have the purpouse of to destroy, alter or change infrastucture.

# Vulnerabilities and attacks 

A _**vulnerability**_ is a weakness or a flaw of a system, function or some internal interaction that can compromise its security helping the attacker to gain access to sensitive data or to control a system from the inside. 

Most of the vulnerabilities that have been discovered are noted _**Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures or CVE**_ database. 

Vulnerabilities can be researched, reverse-engineered, discovered or exploited using special automated tools or custom scripts. 

Various parties or people are vulnerable to cyber attacks, howerver all of the attacks to them will be unique in dependence of who they are. 

_**SMBs (Small and Medium Businesses)**_ are most affected by <span style="color:rgb(255, 0, 0)">malware, ransomware, phishing, Denial-Of-Service (DoS), Man-in-the-middle</span>.  

But most of the people are most likely to be attacked by untargeted attacks.
