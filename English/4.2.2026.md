# Lesson 10 Reading

## Topic: Introduction to IELTS Reading

IELTS Academic reading focuses on:
1. Reading for gist and detail 
2. Understanding opinions, arguments and purposes 
3. Recognizing paraphrasing 

_Attention!_
60 min 
3 long academic texts 
40 questions in total 
aprox. 20 min per text ( no extra time )
Texts might be taken from books, academic journals academic magazines and newspapers 
Topics might include education, history, science and technology, environment, psychology and social issues 
You may be asked to answer the following types of questions: 
1. Multiple choice 
2. True / False / Not given 
3. Yes / No / Not given 
4. Matching headings ( Title )
5. Matching information 
6. Sentence completion 
7. Summary, table or flow chart completion 
8. Short answer questions
Each question test a different reading skill: 
	1. Identify main ideas 
	2. Locate specific information quickly ( scanning )
	3. Understand paraphrasing 
	4. Recognize writer's opinion 
	5. Follow logical argumentation
Each correct answer is 1 point 
The raw score of 40 is converted to the band score from 1 to 9

---

_**Approximate Guide**_

23-26 - Band 6.0
30-32 - Band 7.0
33-34 - Band 7.5
35-36 - Band 8.0
37-38 - Band 8.5
39-40 - Band 9.0

---
_**Major errors to avoid making!**_

- Avoid running out of time
- Avoid reading every word, you better scheme
- Avoid confusing false with not given 
- Avoid missing paraphrases 
- Avoid overthinking simple questions 

---
_Strategies to be applied:_
1. Read the questions first no the whole text 
2. Scheme for structures, scan for answers, underline keywords and paraphrasing 
3. Manage time strictly max 20 min per text 
4. Never leave answers blank!!!
