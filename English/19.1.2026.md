# Lesson 3

_Listening module:_ 
Duration 30 mins, no extra time 
Number of sections = _4_ 
Total questions _40 question_
Audio played will be available only _once only_

Section 1: Social Enviroment (Dialog 2 speakers _easy_)
Section 2: Social Enviroment (Monologue 1 speaker _easy-medium_)
Section 3: Academic Enviroment (Dialog _2-4 speakers medium difficulty_)
Section 4: Academic Enviroment (Lecture 1 speaker _hard_)

The listening module consists of the following questions:
1. Multiple choice (You must choose one correct answer or 2-3 correct answers from a list )
2. Matching ( Match items: people, things, places, theories with options A-F )
3. Plan labeling, Map labeling and Diagram labeling 
4. Form, notes, flow charts and tables
5. Sentence completion ( you will have to complete sentences using information from the recording, sometimes you will need to complete sentence at the beggining or at the end )
6. Short-answer questions 

## What does the listening exam test? 

1. Attention to detail 
2. Attitude 
3. Main idea 
4. Recognition of relationships
5. Ability to follow multiple speakers
6. Understanding of directions
7. Vocabulary related to the direction and movement 
8. Listening for specific information
9. Spelling
10. Grammar accuracy 
11. Paraphrasing
12. Precise listening
13. And the ability to filter irrelevant information
