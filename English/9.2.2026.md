# Lesson 12 Reading

## Topic: Short-Answer and Sentence-Completion Questions 

For this type of questions the instructions are the same

### Tips: 
1. Read the instructions careful. Always check: 
	1. Word limit 
	2. If numbers, dates or symbols count as words 
	3. Required format (word-phrase or number)

2. Identify keywords first. Before reading underline key verbs, nouns, dates, numbers

3. Focus on meaning not the exact wording 

4. Paraphrasing. Answers rarely repeat the exact wording. Common changes: 
	1. children - young people 
	2. buy - purchase
	3. difficult - challenging 

5. Use grammar as a guide to the correct answer: 
	1. Correct verb tense 
	2. Correct singular or plural 
	3. Logical prepositions 

6. Follow word form clues: 
	1. an - is followed by an vowel 
	2. to - is followed by an verb 
	3. very - is followed by an adjective or adverb 

7. Predict before checking 

8. Keep answers precise. No extra words, No unnecessary paraphrasing, No meaning change

9. Check spelling

10. Stay in context.
